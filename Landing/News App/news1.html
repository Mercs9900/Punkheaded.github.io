SpaceX tests engines of most powerful rocket ever built for 1st time

SpaceX conducted a successful ground test of engines of Starship, the most powerful rocket ever built. It test fired 31 of the 33 Super Heavy booster engines for the first time on Thursday. SpaceX CEO Elon Musk tweeted that one engine was turned off before test and another engine "stopped itself", but it was "still enough engines to reach orbit".
